About Us

Most Safe & Rated Hotel In Ksamil.

Set in Ksamil, 700 metres from Ksamil Beach, Hotel ZEKA offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking and a terrace. Built in 2020, the property is within 3 km of Butrint National Park. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, an ATM and free WiFi throughout the property. At the hotel every room includes air conditioning, a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, towels and a balcony with a city view. Certain rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a stovetop. The units will provide guests with a fridge.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at this 3-star hotel. Popular points of interest near Hotel ZEKA include Ksamil Beach 9, Bora Bora Beach and Ksamil Beach 7. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 93 km from the accommodation.
